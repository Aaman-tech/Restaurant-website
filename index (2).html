Our Story

A Culinary Journey
Born from Passion

Founded in 2009, Aurum was born from a simple belief: that exceptional food has the power to create extraordinary moments. Nestled in the heart of the city, our intimate dining room has become a sanctuary for those who appreciate the art of fine cuisine.

Every ingredient is sourced with intention — from the finest wagyu to hand-harvested sea salt. Our philosophy is rooted in classical French technique, elevated with global influences and presented with contemporary elegance.

Chef Laurent Dubois
Executive Chef & Founder
